My name is Johannes Härtel

I am a software engineer and researcher.

About Me

I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Koblenz-Landau, Germany, in 2016, and worked there as a researcher from 2016 to 2022 finishing my Ph.D. Since 2022 I have been working as a postdoc at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB). My special interests include software language engineering, empirical software engineering, statistic modelling, probabilistic programming, functional programming, machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, big-data processing, mining software repositories, testing, and model-driven software engineering techniques.
